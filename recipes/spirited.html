Spirited Away's Onigiri (Rice Ball)

Onigiri from Spirited Away

Chihiro is stuck working at a mystical bath house in hopes of finding her missing parents. She comes across a mysterious boy named Haku who comforts her with new foods.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • salt
  • umeboshi (pickled plum)

Directions

  1. Lay out a sheet of saran wrap.
  2. Lightly salt the saran wrap and place a scoop of hot rice on top.
  3. Flatten the rice to about half an inch.
  4. Place the umeboshi in the center of the rice.
  5. Pick up the saran wrap and gently shape the rice into a triangle.
  6. Remove the rice ball from the saran wrap.
  7. Repeat steps 1 - 6 until out of rice. Enjoy!
