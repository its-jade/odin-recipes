Ponyo's Ham and Ramen

Ham and Ramen from Ponyo

Ponyo is a goldfish princess that longs to become a human. She develops a friendship with a human boy named Sosuke and together they share exciting adventures as well as yummy meals!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant noodles
  • water
  • thick cut ham
  • 1 egg
  • 1 sprig of green onion

Directions

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add egg to boiled water for 8 minutes. Cook for 6 minutes if you prefer a runny yolk.
  3. After 8 minutes peel the boiled egg and set aside.
  4. Cook instant noodles according to package directions.
  5. Chop green onions.
  6. Heat up a pan over medium high heat.
  7. Slice the ham in half and place into hot pan until nicely toasted.
  8. Assemble a bowl with instant ramen, ham slices, egg, and garnish with green onions. Enjoy!
