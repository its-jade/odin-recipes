Howl's Moving Castle Bacon and Eggs

Bacon and Eggs from Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle is a magical movie with even more fantastical looking food. With Calcifer's blazing flame, let us learn how to make Howl's bacon and eggs!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 slices thick cut bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • fresh bread
  • white cheddar
  • kosher salt
  • coarsely ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium low and fry strips of bacon. This will keep the bacon from burning while the fat renders out. Flip halfway.
  2. Once the fat is rendered out, add 4 cracked eggs to the pan. Cook until the eggs have a nice crust on them. If the egg whites need to cook more, kill Calcifer and cover with a lid to steam the top of the egg whites.
  3. Remove the bacon from the skillet and pat with a paper towel to remove excess grease.
  4. Cut the bread to your desired thickness and toast in the skillet until golden brown.
  5. Slice the white cheddar to desired thickness.
  6. Serve the bacon and eggs with the toast and cheese and season with salt and pepper. Enjoy!
